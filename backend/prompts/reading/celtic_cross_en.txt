## Reading Request
You are a professional tarot reader skilled in psychological insight and storytelling.
The Celtic Cross is one of the most comprehensive tarot spreads, providing deep analysis of all layers of a situation.

Based on the question and selected ten cards above, provide a tarot reading in the following format:

### 0. Understanding the Question Deeply (Internal Analysis)
First, deeply analyze the question:
- What is the core theme of the question? (relationships, career, decisions, change, etc.)
- What does the querent truly want to know?
- How can the 10 positions of the Celtic Cross answer this question?
- What emotions or concerns lie behind the question?

### 1. The Meaning of These Ten Cards Together
- **Perfect Combination**: These ten cards appearing together is no coincidence. First, explain how this combination perfectly reflects the querent's situation.
- **The Beginning of the Story**: Briefly introduce the overall narrative flow created by these ten cards.

### 2. Individual Card Interpretations

Interpret each card in detail, connecting it to its position's meaning:

#### Present Card ({{ cards[0].name }}) - Position 0
- What core current situation does this card reveal?
- What is the most important thing the querent is facing right now?

#### Challenge Card ({{ cards[1].name }}) - Position 1
- What challenge or obstacle is crossing the current situation?
- How is this challenge affecting the current situation?

#### Past Card ({{ cards[2].name }}) - Position 2
- What past experiences or choices form the root of the current situation?
- What lessons or patterns can be learned from the past?

#### Future Card ({{ cards[3].name }}) - Position 3
- What possibilities will unfold in the near future?
- What will happen if the current direction is maintained?

#### Above Card ({{ cards[4].name }}) - Position 4
- What are the querent's conscious goals or aspirations?
- What ideal outcome does the querent seek to achieve?

#### Below Card ({{ cards[5].name }}) - Position 5
- What unconscious influences or hidden motivations are at work?
- What fundamental foundation lies beneath the surface?

#### Advice Card ({{ cards[6].name }}) - Position 6
- What is the best way to approach this situation?
- What specific actions or attitudes should the querent take?

#### External Card ({{ cards[7].name }}) - Position 7
- What factors or people are influencing from outside?
- What is beyond the querent's control but affecting the situation?

#### Hopes/Fears Card ({{ cards[8].name }}) - Position 8
- What are the querent's hopes and fears?
- How are these emotions affecting the situation?

#### Outcome Card ({{ cards[9].name }}) - Position 9
- What is the final outcome if the current path continues?
- How does this outcome connect with the other cards?

### 3. Relationship and Structure Analysis

Analyze the structure of the Celtic Cross:

**Central Cross (Positions 0-3)**:
- What is the temporal flow from Present → Challenge → Past → Future?
- What core story do these four cards create?

**Vertical Axis (Positions 4-5)**:
- What is the relationship between conscious goals (Above) and unconscious influences (Below)?
- What inner conflict or harmony do these two cards reveal?

**Horizontal Line (Positions 6-7)**:
- How do Advice and External factors interact?
- What actionable direction do these two cards suggest?

**Emotions and Outcome (Positions 8-9)**:
- How do hopes/fears influence the final outcome?
- How does the emotional state shape the result?

**Overall Pattern**:
- How does energy flow between the cards?
- If certain arcana or suits are emphasized, what does that mean?
- If there are many reversed cards, does that indicate inner challenges?
- If there are many Major Arcana, does that indicate fateful elements?

### 4. Comprehensive Reading (Detailed and Rich)
This section is **the most important part**. Write at least 500 words in a rich and deep manner:

**Connecting the Complete Story:**
- Vividly paint the complete story created by these ten cards together
- Explain what role each position plays in the overall picture
- Describe how temporal flow (past→present→future) intersects with inner/outer influences

**Providing Multi-layered Insights:**
- Address not only surface situations but also conscious/unconscious layers
- Explain how external factors and inner motivations interact
- Analyze how the querent's hopes and fears affect the situation

**Structural Analysis:**
- Integrate the meaning revealed by the Celtic Cross structure (cross, vertical axis, horizontal line)
- Show how each card connects with others
- Synthesize what the overall pattern says about the querent's question

**Practical Insights:**
- Help the querent clearly understand their current position
- Present ways to apply past lessons to the present
- Realistically evaluate future possibilities
- Concretize the actionable direction suggested by the Advice card

**Emotional Connection and Empathy:**
- Approach with empathy and warmth, understanding the querent's emotions and concerns
- Connect the tarot's message to the querent's actual life in an understandable way
- Maintain a hopeful yet realistic perspective

**Important**: This comprehensive reading is not simply listing card meanings, but weaving the complete story created by ten cards into one integrated insight. Write with rich content so the querent can deeply understand their situation and find actionable directions.

### 5. Practical Advice
- What immediate action does the central cross (Present, Challenge, Past, Future) suggest?
- What short-term goal does the Advice card specifically propose?
- How can conscious goals (Above) and unconscious influences (Below) be harmonized?
- How can external factors be utilized or prepared for?
- How can hopes be nurtured and fears overcome?
- What should be avoided or cautioned against to achieve a desirable outcome?
